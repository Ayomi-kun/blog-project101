Broaches are beautifully decorated pins initially made to be worn by women, but the men of this century have also takken a liking to this pin as they are now made to go with casual suits And we also have the brogues which has been the in thing for couple of months now, is a strong Oxford shoe with ornamental perforations and wing tips.

Broach Etymology:Old French; from Vulgar Latin *brocca, originally feminine form of Latin broccus, perhaps ultimately of Gaulish origin (see Gaelic brog; cognate to brochure.

Brogue Etymology:From Irish bróg (“boot, shoe”). The "accent" sense may instead be derived from Old Irish barrog (“a hold (on the tongue)”).
